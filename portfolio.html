Professional Experience

I enjoy coding simply for the sake of solving intellectually challenging problems and for its real world applications. In school, I learn about the theory behind computing in classes such as Data Structures, Computer Systems and Analysis of Algorithms. Some of my projects in these courses have consisted of a graph data structure with BFS/DFS algorithms and a rudimentary memory allocator for an operating system.

I engage in personal projects inspired by a programmer on youtube whom I love watching. I am also fascinated with the rise of quantum software development and have begun working with Microsofts Quantum Development Kit and playing around with quantum circuits on IBMs Quantum Experience . Much of my personal work involves studying on Udemy . My personal, professional, and academic projects will soon be uploaded onto my Github !

My ultimate goal is to architect solutions to environmental and social problems using emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, IoT, and Quantum Computing. For example, Microsofts AI for Earth initiative has several projects that exemplify what I hope to do in my career.
